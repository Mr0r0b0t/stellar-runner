Stellar Runner - Milky Way Drift

Created by VIVEK SINGH · © 2025 · github.com/Mr0r0b0t
Pilot your astronaut through space gates. Collect Stars (+10) and rare glowing Suns (extra shield). Meteoroids streak in later — avoid them!

How to Play

Desktop & Mobile
Credits: Game design & code by VIVEK SINGH. All artwork & audio are generated in-code.
  • Hold Space/↑ (or tap/hold) to thrust upward; release to drift down.
  • Each gate spawns either a Star (+10) or a bright Sun (extra shield) — never both.
  • Meteoroids arrive from the right, limited in number; they move faster at higher scores.
  • Difficulty scales up every 100 points (speed increases, gaps tighten).
  • Mute/unmute with M or the 🔊 button.
