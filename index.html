Stellar Runner - Milky Way Drift

Hold Space/↑ (or tap/hold) to rise; release to drift down. Collect ⭐ Stars (+10), bright ☀️ Suns (shield), avoid meteors and walls.

How to Play

  • Hold Space/↑ (or tap/hold) to thrust upward; release to drift down.
  • Each gate spawns either a Star (+10) or a bright Sun (extra shield).
  • Meteoroids start after 100 points and get faster each +50 points.
  • World speed increases every 50 points.
  • Toggle P or the ⏸/▶️ button to pause/resume.
  • Toggle M or the 🔊 button to mute/unmute audio.
